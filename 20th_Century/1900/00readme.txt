This area contains printable ephemeris files in PDF format.
This format allows to view and print ephemerides in excellent quality.

If you require ephemeris files 
- for different years,
- for a time zone other than Universal Time midnight, 
- for various asteroids, 
- for a zodiac other than tropical
you can create them in the Ephemeris section of Astrodienst's Extended chart
selection: https://www.astro.com/cgi/genchart.cgi?gm=a3

The ephemeris files may be downloaded and printed for private
use only. Any redistribution of these files in electronic or
printed format requires the permission by Astrodienst in writing.
